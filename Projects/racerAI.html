2D Racer AI

This visual/game displays AI learning how to race around a track using a neural network and a genetic algorithm for training. Many of them die off but a few are able to eventually figure out a path to the end!

You can press the space bar once to skip to the next generation. Warning: this will mess up the training process as the AI learn live!
